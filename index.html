How to Use This Map

Finding Your Location

Tap "Find Me" to show your current position on the map. The red dot shows where you are. Keep GPS enabled to track your movement along the trail.

If You Feel Lost

Don't worry. The red dot always shows your real position. Follow the trail line back toward the Contemplative Center (marked on the map).

Offline Use

This map works without internet once loaded. Make sure to open it while you have signal, then it will work anywhere on the property.
